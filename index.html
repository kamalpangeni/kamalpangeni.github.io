About Me

Hi there, thank you for visiting my page. My name is Kamal Pangeni. I am a data scientist with 6+ years of industry experience in data mining, perdictive analytics, and machine learning. My interest is in leveraging cutting edge ML/AI technologies to enhance, augment, and automate company-wide decision making process. Before starting my industy journey, I obtained my doctoral degree in theoretical physics.

Brief Bio

I was born and raised in Nepal. I moved to the United States in 2007 for my undergraduate studies. I did my undergraduate studies in Physics and Mathematics from Brigham Young University in Provo, UT. I then joined Washington University in St. Louis to get my Phd in theoretical physics where I worked with with Prof. Michael Ogilive and Prof. Mark Alford. My doctoral reserach was in the intersection of finite density QCD and nuclear physics.

After my phd, I transitioned into Machine Learning and Data Science. I joined Mercy Hospital in St. Louis as Machine Learning Data Scientist. In 2021 I moved to CVS Health as Senior Data Scientist.
